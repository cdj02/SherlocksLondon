Sherlock's Literary London

Embark on a captivating journey through the pages of Sherlock Holmes' adventures as we unveil the literary tapestry of London. Immerse yourself in the rich narrative of the Sherlock Holmes stories by clicking on markers with each colour representing a different story. Step into the shoes of a detective by exploring crime scenes marked by magnifying glasses, perhaps the surrounding witnesses and policemen can tell you something interesting...
